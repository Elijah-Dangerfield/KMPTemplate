{{APP_NAME}} is designed to collect as little information about you as possible. This page explains what is and isn't collected.

What's stored on your device

The app stores data locally on your phone, and nowhere else. If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

What's sent off your device

The app may send a small amount of information to a third-party service called Sentry when the app crashes or encounters an error. This helps find and fix bugs. The information includes:

  • A crash log (what went wrong, where in the code it happened).
  • Your device model and operating system version.
  • The version of the app you're running.

This data is not tied to your identity.

What isn't collected

The app does not:

  • Use advertising or analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, no Facebook SDK, no ad networks).
  • Track your location.
  • Sell or share any data.
  • Require an account (unless your project adds one).

Permissions the app asks for

  • Notifications (iOS, if granted): used to show timely updates from the app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted here and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised.
